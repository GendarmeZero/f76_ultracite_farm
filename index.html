Detailed Calculations

Base SCORE per Kill (Without Boost)

300,000 XP/kill ÷ 10,000 XP = 30 increments

30 increments × 100 SCORE = 3,000 SCORE per kill

SCORE per Kill (With 40% Boost)

Base SCORE per kill × (1 + Boost Percentage)

3,000 × (1 + 0.40) = 3,000 × 1.40 = 4,200 SCORE per kill

Kills Needed Calculation

Without Boost: 220,275 total SCORE ÷ 3,000 SCORE/kill = 73.425 kills

→ Rounded up: 74 kills needed

With 40% Boost: 220,275 total SCORE ÷ 4,200 SCORE/kill = 52.446 kills

→ Rounded up: 53 kills needed

Important Considerations

Time Investment

Each Ultracite Terror kill requires completing the "Gleaming Depths" raid, which typically takes 20-30 minutes with a competent team. This means:

  • Without boost: 74 kills × 25 minutes ≈ 1,850 minutes (30.8 hours)
  • With 40% boost: 53 kills × 25 minutes ≈ 1,325 minutes (22.1 hours)

The boost saves approximately 8.7 hours of gameplay.

Using the Raid Checkpoints method to farm Snake

How the Snake Farm Method Works ?

You have an alt (a mule account or character) deliberately taken through the raid up to checkpoint 4 ,the stage just before the snake boss.

This alt then serves as the group leader to enter the raid directly at the snake stage then leave before the encounter start.

Step by Step :

  • The group led by the alt starts the raid
  • Before the snake encounter begins, the alt leaves the group (fast travel outside).
  • The remaining team continues and fights the snake.
  • Afterwards, the main character (or others) can rejoin the alt’s saved checkpoint to repeat the process.

    • TIP: After entering the Raid, kill your character to respawn at the snake checkpoint so you won't have to walk all the way down.

Other SCORE Sources

This calculation assumes you're ONLY using the repeatable XP challenge. In practice, you should complete Daily and Weekly challenges:

  • Daily challenges: Up to 2,250 SCORE/day
  • Weekly challenges: Up to 10,000 SCORE/week

These challenges also receive the 40% boost, making them even more valuable.

Difficulty Overview

The Ultracite Terror is the final boss of the "Gleaming Depths" expedition, considered one of the most challenging encounters in Fallout 76. Success requires:

  • High-level characters (ideally level 150+)
  • Optimized builds and gear
  • Team coordination and communication
  • Knowledge of boss mechanics and attack patterns
  • Substantial ammunition and resource investment
